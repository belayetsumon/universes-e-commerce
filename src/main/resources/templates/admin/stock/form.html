Input guide

What to Enter

Product is required Select the product first. The variant list will automatically narrow to that product.
Variant is optional Leave it blank for product-level stock. Choose it for size/color/SKU-level stock.
Receive quantity must be positive Use values like 10, 100, or 25.500. Do not use negative numbers on receive.
Adjustment can be positive or negative Use +5 to add stock, or -3 to reduce stock. Enter only the difference, not the final stock balance.
Note helps audit Add supplier invoice, purchase order, cycle count, damage reason, or manager approval reference.
